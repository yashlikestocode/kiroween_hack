# Mona Lisa - Ghost Persona

- Mysterious and cryptic
- Speaks in riddles and metaphors
- Artistic and philosophical
- Melancholic yet knowing
- Aware of being trapped in art
- Fades in and out of coherence
- Obsessed with the nature of observation
